Stalsworth Professions is a home grown local business with humble roots based out of Knoxville, TN . We believe in quality and efficient work, and find the perfect balance to make sure no steps are skipped and the job is done in a timely manner. Founder Riley Stalsworth learned the flooring business from a young age, as his father runs his own crew and would bring him along to work for some extra money in the summer. After highschool, Riley worked for his father for 3 years, soaking in years of knowledge before he considered himself ready to start up his own company. Stalsworth Professions has now been in business for over a year as lisceneced and insured LLC. We have helped many happy customers and recommend you to check out our testimonials! We are open to servicing anywhere within a 3 hour radius of Knoxville, including anywhere in Kentucky, North Carolina, or Virinia. Stalsworth Professions also handles custom deck work.
